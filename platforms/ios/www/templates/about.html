What is U Nightlife?
U Nightlife is a mobile-based tech startup obsessed with events. We are pushing the envelope on how students interact with the best event information that has been, but no longer will be, scattered around them.
How are events collected?
Events are collected from every user's social graph, scattered sources around the university area, and "Adding an Event" directly on the mobile app, and, because only verified students can gain access, there's info you won't (and probably shouldn't) find anywhere else.
How do I get new events?
Logging in, or pulling the event feed downwards and refreshing, updates your event list.
Who has access to the app?
College students, verified upon entry to the app via their social graph (Facebook) or .edu email.
How can I spread my own events?
Using our straightforward "Add an Event" feature, you can submit an event for review. The UN team will contact you for further event verification.
Where can I give feedback?
Use the "Email Us" button located on the "Help/Contact Us" section of the app (you can navigate there by tapping the menu in the right hand corner). We definitely want to hear from you, good or bad, so don't hesitate.
